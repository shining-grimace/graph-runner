# Opening
## Aim
Make the player feel the aristocratic mystery in their surroundings, and a compulsion to explore the palace. Focus only a single path forward. Evoke questions of who the player is and what they're here for. Movement mechanics required are very basic, running down a series of passages. Paint the Palace as a secure stronghold without explaining the the Artist build it that way in the past out of necessity.
## Details
The player wears what looks like a uniform, but with unusual match-ups. The initial view teases several paths to explore but they're all dead ends. NPCs dressed in uniforms alike to the player's outfit are scattered about but they don't reveal many answers. Walls are covered in artwork and ornate details. A large door is eye-catching but it's locked. The way forward is a downward spiral of passages that anyone can run through but speedrunners could fly through. A picture on the wall of the Artist grabs attention, though the identity is unknown to the player.
## Plot
Sign beside door:
- Beauty Doesn't Create Itself. Please Do Not Disturb.
Cheeky Guard:
- Off for another job? At least wear some respectable shorts.
- (only after the above, low chance) What? Shorts are just so easy to wear. This place shouldn't be so formal.
- Do come back soon. Nobody else around here ever wants to talk to me.
- What does the boss even look like these days? Been so long since he came out of there.
Assorted Guards:
- The buyers are such snobs.
- I don't know why you got chosen to run these jobs, but thanks to you we all get paid. So, thanks!
- Do watch your step through the exit. I know you've done this before, but it's a doozy.
- Any chance I can see the piece you're carrying this time?
- Sometimes I wonder if the boss gets help with his work.

# A Contrasting World
## Aim
Introduce a community existing in contrast to the palace. Evoke questions of why the player wears elements similar to this community. The mood slows down, becoming sparse and sombre. The first real threats are introduced, but starting simple and with breathing room.
## Details
Leaving the palace door, a series of pits tests some basic platforming in a forgiving way. The landscape runs down an incline into a valley reveals itself to be an expansive, inhospitable wasteland dotted with unappealing shacks showing signs of lived life. Many broken constructions dot the landscape. A massive construction gives access up a cliff face to reach the main town.
## Plot
Assorted signs:
- ABANDONED - Please keep out
- FOR SALE
Assorted townsfolk:
- We keep to ourselves here and live a simple life
- Guk, I lost it all on the badgers.
Humble Builder:
- There's no way I can keep up with all the work in this place. But it's what I love doing.
- The tower by the cliff is on my to-do list.
- I hope you find meaning in your work. It's certainly not a conventional role, at least when you look at history.
- I couldn't stand living alone any more. I'm needed here. Really needed.
Signs by the cliff:
- Maintenance in Progress: Keep Out
- Locals have reported individuals climbing these structures. Offenders are pursued for prosecution.
